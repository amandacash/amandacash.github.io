About Me
Hi! My name is Amanda Cash! I am currently a senior at the University of Maryland College Park, majoring in journalism with a concentration in public policy. I was a student at Towson University for two years and then transferred to UMD in my junior year of college. At University of Maryland, I am the president of Terps for Service Members, the website manager for UMUC radio and a volunteer at the Capital News Service.
